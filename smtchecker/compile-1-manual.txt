@ For the CST sqrt emission V2 formula, prefer the direct solc harness path first.
  This avoids asking Hardhat to compile and model-check the entire project.

  Example:

  HARDHAT_MODE_CODE=1 \
  ENABLE_HARDHAT_PREPROCESSOR=true \
  ENABLE_ASSERTS=true \
  ENABLE_SMTCHECKER=2 \
  SMTCHECKER_ENGINE=bmc \
  SMTCHECKER_TIMEOUT_MS=10000 \
  SMTCHECKER_DIRECT_SOLC_FILE='contracts/tests/CstRewardCalculatorSmtHarness.sol' \
  SMTCHECKER_DIRECT_SOLC_CONTRACT='CstRewardCalculatorSmtHarness' \
  python3 smtchecker/compile-1.py

  This proves the bounded arithmetic that feeds the CST reward square root.
  A full `CosmicSignatureGameV2` SMT pass is much heavier and can time out,
  especially because OpenZeppelin `Math.sqrt` is difficult for the solver.

@ To attempt a targeted Hardhat SMT pass anyway, set:

  SMTCHECKER_CONTRACTS='contracts/production/CosmicSignatureGameV2.sol:CosmicSignatureGameV2'
  SMTCHECKER_ENGINE=bmc
  SMTCHECKER_TIMEOUT_MS=10000

  and execute './compile-1.bash' or 'python3 compile-1.py'.
